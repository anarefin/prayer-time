# Privacy Policy for Jamaat at Masjid App

**Effective Date:** December 30, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Jamaat at Masjid ("the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application for accessing prayer times and mosque information.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information
- **Email Address:** When you create an account, we collect your email address for authentication purposes
- **User Account Information:** We store a unique user identifier (UID) and assign a user role (regular user or admin)

### 2. App Usage Information
- **Favorite Mosques:** We keep track of mosques you've marked as favorites to provide a personalized experience
- **Location Data:** The app requests access to your location to show nearby mosques and relevant prayer times. This data is processed locally on your device and is not stored on our servers
- **Device Information:** Basic device information may be collected to optimize app performance

### 3. Technical Information
- **App Usage Analytics:** Non-personal information about how you interact with the app may be collected to improve our services
- **Device Identifiers:** We may collect device identifiers for the purpose of delivering notifications

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

1. **Account Management:** To create and manage your user account
2. **Service Delivery:** To provide prayer times and mosque information
3. **Personalization:** To save your favorite mosques and preferences
4. **Notifications:** To send prayer time reminders (if enabled)
5. **App Improvement:** To analyze usage patterns and improve our services
6. **Customer Support:** To respond to your questions and provide technical support

## Data Storage and Security

- Your email address and account information are stored securely using Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore
- Your favorite mosques list is stored in your user profile
- Location data is used in real-time and is not permanently stored on our servers
- We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

1. **Service Providers:** With trusted third-party service providers who assist in operating our app (Firebase services)
2. **Legal Requirements:** If required by law, court order, or government regulation
3. **Business Transfer:** In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

## Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

1. **Access:** You can access the personal information we hold about you
2. **Correction:** You can update your account information through the app
3. **Deletion:** You can request deletion of your account and associated data
4. **Portability:** You can request a copy of your data in a structured format

## Contact Us

Email: privacy@jamaatatmasjid.com

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in this location and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.

## Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). These include the right to know what personal information is being collected, the right to delete personal information, and the right to opt-out of the sale of personal information. Note that we do not sell personal information.

## Location Services

The app uses your location to provide nearby mosque information. Location services are optional, and you can choose to disable them in your device settings. If you disable location services, you can still use the app, but certain features may be limited.

## Notifications

The app can send prayer time notifications. You can manage your notification preferences in the app settings or your device settings. We do not use notification data for any other purpose than to provide prayer time reminders.

## Local Storage

Some data may be stored locally on your device to improve app performance and provide offline functionality. This includes cached prayer times and your favorite mosques list. You can clear this data through the app settings.
