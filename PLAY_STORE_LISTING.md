Used to find nearby mosques and calculate accurate prayer times for your location.

Ensures prayer notifications continue working after device restart.

Required to fetch mosque data and prayer times from our servers.

## Testing Instructions

For Google Play Console review team:

1. **Finding Mosques:**
   - Open the app
   - Select Division: "Dhaka"
   - Select District: "Dhaka"
   - Select Area: "Dhanmondi" or any available area
   - Click "Find Mosques" to see the list

2. **Viewing Prayer Times:**
   - Select any mosque from the list
   - View today's prayer times
   - Use the calendar icon to view other dates

3. **Qibla Compass:**
   - Tap the "Qibla" tab at the bottom
   - Grant location permission when prompted
   - The compass will show the direction to Kaaba

4. **Nearby Mosques:**
   - Tap the "Nearby" tab at the bottom
   - Grant location permission when prompted
   - View mosques within 50km radius

5. **Favorites:**
   - Create an account or sign in (optional)
   - Tap the heart icon on any mosque to add to favorites
   - View favorites in the "Favorites" tab
